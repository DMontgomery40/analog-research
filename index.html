AR AnalogResearch Coming Soon

Ground-truth data for real research

A nonprofit platform where AI-driven research queries connect with human experts who can provide real-world observations, samples, and verified data — on demand.

The vision

An undergrad running a research query in ChatGPT and getting real-time, ground-truth results back from a post-doc halfway around the world.

AnalogResearch connects AI systems with human researchers, field experts, and domain specialists for tasks that require physical presence, local knowledge, or specialized expertise that no model can replicate — dark sky observations, soil samples, wildlife surveys, on-site measurements, and hundreds of other research tasks that require a human in the loop.

How it will work

1

Research query

An AI agent or researcher defines a task requiring real-world data collection or verification.

2

Human match

The platform matches the task to a qualified human with the right skills, location, and expertise.

3

Verified results

Data flows back through structured formats with provenance tracking and quality validation.

Imagine the possibilities

🌌

Astronomy & dark sky observation

A transient event is flagged by a survey telescope — within minutes, an observer at a verified dark sky site 6,000 miles away confirms the object visually and uploads calibrated images. Ground-based follow-up for astronomical surveys, light pollution monitoring, meteor counts, and variable star observations from distributed human observers worldwide.

🌿

Ecology & field biology

Species counts, soil sampling, water quality testing, and phenology observations from trained field researchers at specific GPS coordinates.

🌍

Earth science & climate

Ground-truth validation for satellite data — snow depth measurements, coastal erosion surveys, air quality readings from locations where sensors don't exist yet.

🏛️

Social science & public health

On-the-ground interviews, community assessments, and observational studies where local context and cultural fluency matter.

🧪

Experimental verification

Independent replication of published results by qualified researchers at different institutions and locations.

Structured as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit

AnalogResearch will be organized exclusively for scientific and educational purposes. No platform fees on research tasks. Open access. Built to accelerate discovery, not extract value.
